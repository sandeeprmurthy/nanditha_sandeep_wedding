Things to Do

If you’re traveling from out of town, we hope you’ll get to explore Bangalore a little while you’re here.

  • Coffee in Indiranagar or Koramangala.
  • Walk through Cubbon Park in the early morning.
  • Visit one of the city’s many bookstores and cafes.
  • Enjoy a dosa, filter coffee, and some local sweets.
