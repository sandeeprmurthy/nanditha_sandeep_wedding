Q + A

What should I wear?

Think festive and comfortable. Traditional Indian attire or semi-formal Western outfits are both welcome.

Can I bring a plus one?

If your invitation mentions a guest by name, they are warmly invited! If you’re unsure, feel free to reach out.

Are children invited?

We love your little ones, but due to space we’re keeping the event mostly adults-only, with exceptions noted on specific invitations.

Will there be transportation?

We plan to provide group transportation between the main hotel and the ceremony. Details will be shared closer to the date.

Things to Do

If you’re traveling from out of town, we hope you’ll get to explore Bangalore a little while you’re here.

  • Coffee in Indiranagar or Koramangala.
  • Walk through Cubbon Park in the early morning.
  • Visit one of the city’s many bookstores and cafes.
  • Enjoy a dosa, filter coffee, and some local sweets.
